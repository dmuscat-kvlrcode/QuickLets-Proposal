§ 02 - Problem Statement

A structural bottleneck.

QuickLets relies on manual outreach to confirm property availability - a process that works, but has become a ceiling on growth. As the portfolio expands, the overhead scales linearly with it.

In practice, agents spend a significant share of their day placing individual calls to property owners, chasing unanswered calls, transcribing responses, and updating the CRM by hand. The cost is felt across four dimensions:

01 - Capacity

Agent time

Hours that should go into client-facing work and active deal management are absorbed by repetitive administrative outreach. As the listing base expands, this overhead scales linearly.

02 - Accuracy

Data freshness

Availability is checked reactively rather than systematically. Listings drift out of date - leading to wasted viewings and avoidable friction with prospective tenants.

03 - Integrity

CRM trust

Manual updates introduce inconsistencies and delays. The CRM - which should be the single source of truth - ends up trailing the reality on the ground.

04 - Experience

Owner friction

Property owners receive repeated, uncoordinated calls asking the same questions. This erodes trust, strains the relationship, and risks disengagement from owners who feel their time isn't respected.

The underlying issue is not the tooling but the absence of a structured, automated layer between QuickLets, the Property Owner, and the CRM. That gap is what this proposal addresses.

§ 03 - Proposed Solution

A purpose-built AI agent for full automation.

KVLR Code proposes the design, build, and deployment of an AI Agent that automates WhatsApp outreach to handle Property Owner availability checks end-to-end - and synchronises the results back into QuickLets' CRM.

The solution operates as a structured, three-stage workflow:

01

Triggered outreach

The AI Agent initiates a WhatsApp conversation from a QuickLets-approved business number, based on rules agreed during design. Primary triggers are stale listings (no trusted confirmation within the freshness window) and explicit manual triggers from an authorised agent. Each message is templated, branded, and personalised per owner using CRM data - referencing the specific property, last known status, and the question being asked. Outreach is invoked only after any internal verification path has been exhausted.

02

Conversational data capture

The AI Agent guides the owner through a short, natural exchange to confirm whether the property is still available, surface any changes in price or conditions, and capture free-text notes where relevant. Conversations are conducted in English and designed to feel human, not robotic. Any case the system cannot confidently resolve is passed to a human agent to handle manually.

03

CRM write-back

Once the owner's response is captured, structured data - availability status, changed fields, source (Owner WhatsApp), timestamp, and full conversation transcript - is written back into the CRM against the correct property and owner records. Where a captured outcome materially contradicts the existing CRM state, the agent flags it for manual review rather than silently overwriting. Agents receive a notification only when human attention is genuinely required.

§ 04 - Technical Overview

Architecture & design.

The agent is built as a focused, event-driven service that sits between two external systems - WhatsApp and QuickLets' CRM. It is deliberately narrow in scope and high in leverage, organised around three functional pillars with security and governance embedded across all of them.

01 - AI Agent

Purpose-built for a single job: confirming property availability. A finite-state conversation engine controls flow (greeting, question, clarification, confirmation, close), while a language understanding layer maps free-text replies to a defined set of structured outcomes:

  • Available now
  • Not available
  • Available from a future date (date captured)
  • Conditions changed
  • No reply
  • Escalate to human

Outcomes outside this set are never inferred - they are escalated. Where an owner does not reply, the agent follows a configurable reminder cadence before closing the conversation and recording it as no reply. A deliberate cap on outreach protects owners from feeling repeatedly chased.

Where an owner's reply materially contradicts the current CRM state, the agent does not silently overwrite the record. It flags the conversation for manual review with full context attached - protecting data integrity in exactly the cases where a wrong automatic update is most costly.

02 - CRM Integration

The CRM remains the single source of truth. Integration is handled through a newly built API layer, purpose-built to ensure a clean and maintainable connection between the agent and QuickLets' existing systems. A dedicated integration adapter reads from the CRM to drive outreach and writes back to it as conversations resolve. Every completed conversation produces a structured update including:

  • Availability status (available / not available / available later / no reply)
  • Available-from date where captured
  • Any changed fields (e.g. price or conditions)
  • Source recorded as "Owner WhatsApp"
  • Timestamp of confirmation
  • Attempt count and last contact attempt date
  • Full conversation transcript for audit

Writes are idempotent and auditable. Every change carries a clear lineage back to the conversation that produced it. Status fields and custom properties are used in preference to free-text notes, so the data is queryable and reportable.

03 - WhatsApp Integration

All messaging operates from a QuickLets-verified WhatsApp Business Account. Integration adheres to Meta's Business Messaging Policy, template approval requirements, and the boundaries between business-initiated and user-initiated conversation windows.

Hosting

The system resides within QuickLets' own environment, hosted alongside the QuickLets CRM. Data residency stays aligned; no cross-environment transfers. KVLR Code holds scoped operational access to deploy, monitor, and maintain the service. QuickLets retains ownership of the underlying infrastructure.

Security, compliance & governance

Data Protection

Encryption & access

All personal data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Role-based access control with full audit logging on every read and write.

Regulation

GDPR & EU AI Act

Lawful basis recorded. Retention periods configurable and enforced automatically. Defined workflow for data subject requests within statutory timeframes.

Audit

Immutable log

Every conversation and every CRM write is logged immutably. Clear, queryable record of what the system did, when, and on whose behalf.
